## Buyer-fit questions

1. Is typing noise acceptable if coworkers or housemates are nearby?
2. Do you prefer compact TKL/75% layouts or full size with numpad?
3. Is hot-swap flexibility important for future switch experiments?
4. For mice, is claw, palm, or fingertip grip non-negotiable?
5. Will wireless latency matter for competitive FPS play?
6. Does desk depth constrain keyboard and mouse pad placement together?
7. Are you pairing peripherals with a specific monitor refresh workflow?
8. Is office typing feel more important than RGB and gaming features?
