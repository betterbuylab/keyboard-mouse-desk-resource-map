# Buyer-fit checklist

- [ ] Is typing noise acceptable if coworkers or housemates are nearby?
- [ ] Do you prefer compact TKL/75% layouts or full size with numpad?
- [ ] Is hot-swap flexibility important for future switch experiments?
- [ ] For mice, is claw, palm, or fingertip grip non-negotiable?
- [ ] Will wireless latency matter for competitive FPS play?
- [ ] Does desk depth constrain keyboard and mouse pad placement together?
- [ ] Are you pairing peripherals with a specific monitor refresh workflow?
- [ ] Is office typing feel more important than RGB and gaming features?
